Introduction

Recipe manager is an application for learning and sharing new recipes. With recipe manager, users will never lose their recipes again. Recipe manager allows their users to synchronize their accounts with multiple devices. Now users can easily upload, update, and delete their recipes. Recipe manager also supports text, sound, and video formats. Users can upload their recipes and give directions in the text, sound, and video format. Users will be logged on the application as an Amateur cook, Home Cook or Pro Chef. This application also gives a chance to users to interact with each other. As an example, an Amateur Cook can follow a Pro Chef and have a chance to chat with them or they can just use their recipes. Recipes will be categorized in different forms. Such as, difficulty level, food category and preparation time so that users can easily find the recipes they need. Recipe manager is also great for users who can't decide what to eat. With this application, users can choose what to cook from recipe managers weekly meal suggestions.
